Forgotten Streets: The Voices of Lost Childhood

Content Type: Documentary film

Logline

A heartfelt documentary that reveals the everyday battles, dreams, and resilience of children living on the streets, fighting to survive in a world that often refuses to see them.

Promotional Spiel

Forgotten Streets: The Voices of Lost Childhood brings viewers face-to-face with the unseen reality of street-dwelling children - kids who deserve safety, education, and love, yet grow up surrounded by struggle instead of support. This documentary aims to spark awareness and empathy by highlighting their daily challenges: searching for food, protection, and a place to belong.

Through raw storytelling and real-life accounts, the film explores the reasons they end up in the streets - poverty, broken families, neglect ‚Äî while uncovering their quiet hope for a brighter tomorrow.
